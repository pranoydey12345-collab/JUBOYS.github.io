5. Laboratory Conclusion Summary

Resonant Parameters Analyzed:

The RLC circuit resonates near its theoretical resonating frequency.

Analytical Conclusion Checklist:
  • Half-Sinusoidal Waves: Evaluates both fundamental and even terms(f0 ,f0/2,f0/4).
  • Square and triangular waves contain only odd harmonics .
  • Square wave: odd harmonics decrease as 1/n.
  • Triangular wave: odd harmonics decrease much faster, as 1/n^2 .
