About Project

The importance of our project to provide a low-cost air quality monitoring system. Today, air pollution has become a global issue and people are suffering from various diseases (eg. Asthma, COPD, Bronchitis etc.) caused by poor air quality. Many harmful gases, PM 2.5 particles and PM 10 particles present in the atmosphere are responsible for air pollution.Tracking these various air quality parameters in real time will help us monitor air quality and take necessary steps further. The AQI is a standard tool to identify the present scenario of air quality.
